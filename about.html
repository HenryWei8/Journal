    About Us

    The Fiat Lux Journal is an open-access, peer-reviewed, nonprofit journal dedicated to supporting students in developing research skills and earning recognition for academic excellence. Our editorial board consists of a panel of published authors and exceptional post-secondary students with extensive backgrounds in scientific research and writing.
    As our organization is dedicated to supporting aspiring researchers, we will guide students through the publication process from scratch, then offer an opportunity to publish with us.

    Our Mission

    We aim to democratize essential knowledge by providing accessible research opportunities for all interested students.

    Scope

    The Fiat Lux Journal is a multidiscplinary research journal. We invite original thesis and research in any academic field. We accept manuscripts including but not limited to:

    • Original research that enhances the existing scientific knowledge base, encompassing interdisciplinary efforts, findings that may challenge prevailing views, or studies aimed at reproducing prior results.
    • Qualitative investigations that adhere to recognized reporting standards and uphold rigorous ethical and methodological criteria.
    • Opinion essays that provide a concise overview and critical evaluation of a specific scientific matter.
    • Review articles (e.g.literature, systematic) characterized by methodologies that guarantee a thorough and impartial examination of the available body of literature.

    Why Publish to The Fiat Lux Journal

    We are welcoming all students regardless of research background and experience. We teach students research skills starting from scratch and help them acheive a standard deserving recognition from the global audience.
