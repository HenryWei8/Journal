• 01

    Abstract

    Maximum 250 words; summarize aim and scope, methods, results, conclusions, and implications of the study; written in plain language.

  • 02

    Introduction

    To provide context and background for readers; include research question(s), variables, objective, importance, and relevance.

  • 03

    Results/Discussion

    Outcomes presented in tables and figures (images/graphs); description and interpretation of outcomes with focus on observations, patterns, and trends; summarize significance of findings and explain significance.

  • 04

    Citations and References

    All information and data from other sources must be cited in text and in a reference list at the end of the paper. Citation style is left to authors' discretion.
